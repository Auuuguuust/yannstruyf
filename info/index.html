Yann Struyf is a painter who translates his personal environment and interests into his medium, paint. This environment is captured with a camera or sketchbook to serve as a starting point. In the photos, he searches for a painterly quality that is yet to be brought to life. He focuses on what is present through its absence or deliberate removal from perception. In doing so, he attempts to shift and open up the weight of the image.
